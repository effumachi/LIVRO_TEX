Questões

1. Determine \(x\) e \(y\) de modo que as matrizes abaixo sejam iguais: \[\label{key} \mathbb{A} = \left[ \begin{matrix} 1&2 \\ 4&x+5 \end{matrix} \right] \qquad \mathbb{B} = \left[ \begin{matrix} y-3&2 \\ 4&10 \end{matrix} \right]\]

2. Calcule \(\mathbb{A} \cdot \mathbb{B}\) sendo \(\mathbb{A} = \left[ \begin{matrix} 1&2 \\ 4&2 \end{matrix} \right]\) e \(\mathbb{B} = \left[ \begin{matrix} 0&2 \\ 3&1 \end{matrix} \right]\)

3. Calcule \(2 \mathbb{A} +3 \mathbb{B}\) sendo \(\mathbb{A} = \left[ \begin{matrix} -1&0 \\ 1&2 \end{matrix} \right]\) e \(\mathbb{B} = \left[ \begin{matrix} 0&2 \\ 1&-1 \end{matrix} \right]\)

4. Calcule \(\mathbb{A} - \mathbb{B}\) sendo \(\mathbb{A} = \left[ \begin{matrix} 4&5 \\ 0&2 \end{matrix} \right]\) e \(\mathbb{B} = \left[ \begin{matrix} 0&2 \\ 3&1 \end{matrix} \right]\)

5. Calcule \(\mathrm{det}(\mathbb{A})\) sendo \(\mathbb{A} = \left[ \begin{matrix} 1&2 \\ 4&2 \end{matrix} \right]\).

6. Calcule \(\mathrm{det}(\mathbb{A})\) sendo \(\mathbb{A} = \left[ \begin{matrix} 1&2&3 \\ 4&2&1 \\ -1&2&0 \end{matrix} \right]\).

7. Calcule \(\mathrm{det}(\mathbb{A})\) sendo \(\mathbb{A} = \left[ \begin{matrix} 1&3&0&1 \\ 4&2&0&0 \\ -1&3&0&1 \\ 4&3&2&0 \end{matrix} \right]\).
